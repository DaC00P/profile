Hi! I'm Daniel!

My first day as a QA Tester, I brought down a live server. Even before the sysadmin ran to my desk, telling me to stop whatever I was doing - while at the same time praising me for finding the oddest of recursion bugs - I had a love for breaking systems. That has remained, but I also developed a powerful passion for building, architecting, and testing as a Software Engineer. I am a strong believer in collaboration, testing (and breaking) code, and problem solving. I work on the entire stack, from deploying Rails to AWS Elastic Beanstalk with Devise & OAuth, to structuring and styling React components on the Front End.
